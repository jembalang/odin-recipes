Egg Tofu Soup

Description

My favorite wife cooked meal. A simple Chinese Cuisine consisting the main ingredient-egg tofu, flour, some eggs and water.

Best served in rainy weather with jasmine rice and some fried shallots.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of egg tofu
  • 1-2 Tbsp corn flour
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 egg
  • Corn flour to coat egg tofu
  • 3 Tbps cooking oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • chives
  • 2 Tbps oyster sauce
  • Carrots
  • 3 Cups of water
  • Shitake Mushroom
  • or Shimeji Mushroom
  • corn flour water mixture
  • 1/2 Cube of chicken stock
  • Salt as needed
  • Brocoli
  • 3 small green chillies
  • 1 Red chilli

Instructions

  1. Cut egg tofus in about 1 1/2cm of width each
  2. Cut brocolis.
  3. Julliard cut the carrots.
  4. Rough cut the garlic.
  5. Small cut for all the chillies.
  6. Wash and Rinse the mushromms. Pat dry.
  7. Cut the Mushrooms into big pieces.
  8. Seperate the chives' stem(will be used in soup) and leaves(will be used as garnish).
  9. Prepare a mixture of corn flour(1-2 Tbsp) and water (1/4 cups).
  10. Crack the egg and add a bit of water. Mix well together and set aside.
  11. Coat the egg tofu with corn flour.
  12. Heat a skillet and fry the egg tofu until golden brown. Set aside when done.
  13. In a large wok, heat up some cooking oil.
  14. Place in the garlic and cook until golden brown.
  15. Place in the chive stem.
  16. Add oyster sauce and carrot. Mix until 10-15 seconds and then add water.
  17. Add Shitake mushrooms
  18. Cook until mixture boils.
  19. Crack some eggs and drop it into the soup using the egg drop technique.
  20. Add brocolis, chillies and chives.
  21. Lastly, add egg tofu that was fried just now.
  22. Serve in a bowl with a bit of fried shallots.
